Remote Work Impact

Remote positions typically offer:

  • • 10-15% lower than top-tier cities
  • • Location-based adjustments common
  • • More flexibility in negotiations
  • • Additional benefits to offset salary
  • • Growing trend toward location-agnostic pay

Negotiation Tips

Do's

  • ✓ Research market rates thoroughly before negotiating
  • ✓ Consider total compensation, not just base salary
  • ✓ Practice your negotiation conversation beforehand
  • ✓ Be prepared to justify your requested salary
  • ✓ Get everything in writing before accepting

Don'ts

  • ✗ Don't share your current salary unless required
  • ✗ Don't accept the first offer immediately
  • ✗ Don't make ultimatums or threats
  • ✗ Don't negotiate via email if possible
  • ✗ Don't forget to negotiate benefits and perks
