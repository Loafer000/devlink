Resume Writing Guide

1. Choose the Right Format

Chronological, functional, or combination - pick the format that best showcases your experience

2. Write a Compelling Summary

Start with a powerful 2-3 sentence summary highlighting your key achievements and skills

3. Quantify Your Achievements

Use numbers and metrics to demonstrate your impact (e.g., "Increased sales by 35%")

4. Tailor for Each Job

Customize your resume for each application using keywords from the job description

Interview Preparation

Common Questions

  • • Tell me about yourself
  • • Why do you want this job?
  • • What are your strengths?
  • • Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
  • • Why should we hire you?

Behavioral Questions

  • • Describe a challenge you faced
  • • Tell me about a conflict
  • • Share a leadership example
  • • Discuss a failure
  • • Explain a team success

Technical Questions

  • • Coding challenges
  • • System design
  • • Problem-solving scenarios
  • • Technical case studies
  • • Domain-specific questions

Interview Tips

Before the Interview

  • ✓ Research the company thoroughly
  • ✓ Prepare your STAR stories
  • ✓ Practice with mock interviews
  • ✓ Prepare questions to ask
  • ✓ Plan your outfit and route

During the Interview

  • ✓ Arrive 10-15 minutes early
  • ✓ Make strong eye contact
  • ✓ Listen carefully to questions
  • ✓ Be specific with examples
  • ✓ Ask thoughtful questions
