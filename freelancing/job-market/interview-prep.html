Interview Preparation

Master your interview skills and land your dream job

Common Interview Questions

General Questions

  • • Tell me about yourself
  • • Why do you want this job?
  • • What are your strengths?
  • • What are your weaknesses?
  • • Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
  • • Why should we hire you?
  • • Why are you leaving your current job?

Behavioral Questions

  • • Describe a challenge you faced
  • • Tell me about a conflict at work
  • • Share a leadership example
  • • Discuss a failure and what you learned
  • • Explain a team success
  • • Describe handling a difficult customer
  • • Tell me about meeting a tight deadline

Technical Questions

  • • Coding challenges
  • • System design problems
  • • Problem-solving scenarios
  • • Technical case studies
  • • Domain-specific questions
  • • Algorithm explanations
  • • Project walkthrough requests

The STAR Method

Use this framework to answer behavioral interview questions effectively

S

Situation

Set the context by describing the situation or challenge you faced

T

Task

Explain your responsibility or what needed to be accomplished

A

Action

Describe the specific actions you took to address the situation

R

Result

Share the outcomes and what you learned from the experience

Interview Tips & Best Practices

Before the Interview

  • ✓

    Research the Company

    Study their mission, values, products, and recent news

  • ✓

    Prepare Your Stories

    Have 5-7 STAR examples ready covering different scenarios

  • ✓

    Practice Mock Interviews

    Rehearse with friends or use online platforms

  • ✓

    Prepare Questions

    Have 3-5 thoughtful questions about the role and company

  • ✓

    Plan Your Outfit

    Choose professional attire appropriate for the company culture

During the Interview

  • ✓

    Arrive Early

    Be there 10-15 minutes before your scheduled time

  • ✓

    Make Eye Contact

    Show confidence and engagement through body language

  • ✓

    Listen Carefully

    Take a moment to think before answering questions

  • ✓

    Be Specific

    Use concrete examples and quantify achievements

  • ✓

    Ask Questions

    Show genuine interest in the role and company
